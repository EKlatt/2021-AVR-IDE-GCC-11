I appreciate the work of Zak Kemble and his toolchain:
https://blog.zakkemble.net/avr-gcc-builds/
The contents is find in the folder ..\IDE\toolchain.
This great implementation contains e.g. avr-gcc.exe but alse make.exe,
avrdude.com, avrdude.conf and all the libraries.

1. Download https://blog.zakkemble.net/download/avr-gcc-10.1.0-x64-windows.zip
2. Unpack avr-gcc-10.1.0-x64-windows.zip
3. Find the folder ..\avr-gcc-10.1.0-x64-windows
4. Copy (only the contents) from "avr-gcc-10.1.0-x64-windows" to ..\IDE\toolchain

In consequence of this you get the following structure:

MyIDE
  |
  + -- toolchain
           |
	   + -- avr
           |
           + -- bin		   
           |
	   + -- include
           |
	   + -- lib
	   |
	   + -- libexec
           |
	   + -- share
